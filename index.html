Controls:


Left Arrow - Move Left
Right Arrow - Move Right
Up Arrow - Jump
A key - Attack Left
D key - Attack Right
W key - Attack Up
S key - Attack Down

While on top of a button, press E to activate the button.

You can jump off of spike as long as you're not on them for longer than roughly a second
You can also climb walls or platforms by running into them and then jumping (don't hold the left or right arrow key)

You can push boxes around to gain more height. You may need to jump on a spike and hit the box at the same time at some point ;)

Find a possible bug or have a suggestion? Make an issue on the Github Repository!
Thanks for playing!
